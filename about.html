About Us

At Paradime Motors, we’ve spent the last five years building a reputation for excellence in the world of automotive restoration. Our passion for vintage cars and motors runs deep, and our team’s wealth of knowledge and expertise ensures that every vehicle that enters our shop is treated with the utmost care and precision.

Founded with the goal of preserving the rich history and timeless beauty of classic vehicles, Paradime Motors has quickly become a trusted name in the industry. While our company may be young, our technicians bring over 15 years of experience in automotive restoration, making us true experts in the field. Each technician on our team has honed their craft through years of working on everything from muscle cars and luxury vehicles to rare vintage motors.

We combine modern restoration techniques with traditional craftsmanship to deliver results that not only meet but exceed our customers’ expectations. Our approach is simple: every project is treated as if it were our own. Whether you're looking for a full restoration or a touch-up, our team works closely with each client to ensure we bring their vision to life.

We specialize in mechanical rebuilds, bodywork, interior restoration, custom modifications, and everything in between. No job is too big or small, and we take pride in the quality and attention to detail we put into every project. At Paradime Motors, we believe that restoring a car is more than just fixing mechanical issues; it’s about preserving the spirit of the vehicle. Whether it's the rumble of an engine, the gleam of freshly restored chrome, or the feeling of smooth leather beneath your fingers, we understand what makes each car special and we are committed to reviving that magic.

What sets us apart is our deep appreciation for the art of restoration. We don’t just repair cars; we bring history back to life. Our goal is to ensure that your classic car continues to shine for years to come, driving through the ages as a testament to craftsmanship and timeless design.

Whether you’re a collector looking to restore a rare gem or simply a car enthusiast who wants to relive the glory days of your vehicle, Paradime Motors is here to make your dreams a reality. With our unparalleled experience and passion for classic cars, we’ll give your vehicle the attention and expertise it deserves. Let us show you what makes Paradime Motors different — where expertise, passion, and craftsmanship meet to create automotive masterpieces.
